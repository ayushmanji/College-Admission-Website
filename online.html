Online Payment

Warning:

Please be cautious while making online payments.

1. Ensure that you are on a secure website before entering your payment details.

2. Verify the website's security certificate and look for "https://" in the URL.

3. Avoid entering your payment information on public or unsecured Wi-Fi networks.

4. Double-check the payment details before confirming the transaction.

5. Keep your computer and devices protected with up-to-date antivirus software.

Remember, your online security is important. If you suspect any fraudulent activity, contact our support immediately.
